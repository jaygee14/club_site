About Us

Music Lovers is a non-profit organization that works with and helps independent artists gain a following. Many musicians look to Music Lovers to provide affordable services such as:

  • Studio time
  • Management
  • Rehearsal space
  • Advertising

our goal is to inspire musicians to create music to their highest capabilities as well as providing comfortable, low pressure environments for maximum quality.
